Advanced Dashboard Table for Space Management on Container Ship

2023

Designing a fresh data table tailored for the office manager's use in container ship management has been one of the most enjoyable projects I've worked on in Space Management.

This new table allows them to track and monitor their space's progress as well as additional granular data, all in one convenient location. With the ability to add custom columns and hide default columns that aren't relevant.

Building a modular and scalable dashboard where users can track various data points, from workflow data to custom data , and hide default data that aren't relevant

Additionally, I partnered with products to identify and categorize various column types that would be accessible to our users. We defined how these columns behave and what the user experience looks like.

As the sole product designer on this initiative, I owned the design execution of the entire project, including:

  • Planning and conducting user research, competitor analysis, and broader problem space research for additional user insights
  • Reviewing and validating solutions with stakeholders and users
  • Translating concepts into prototypes and mockups ready for development
  • Defining and detailing user interactions and design logic
  • Collaborating closely with our engineers and QA during development and testing stages
