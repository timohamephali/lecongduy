About

I'm a self-taught designer who finds joy in crafting meaningful, simple digital experiences through a goal-driven and data-informed approach. I spent the first half of my life in Saigon and the other half in Long Thanh town, and now I'm based in sunny Long Thanh town, Vietnam

When I'm not drawing rectangles and writing docs, I'm probably at my favorite local coffee shop keeping up with my journal, or catching up with my friends and my partner as we try the latest restaurant in town.

I could say I stumbled upon design when I started to tinker with HTML/CSS and filmmaking two decade ago, which I always give credit to for my passion for design and user experience. To me, they're merely different mediums used to tell a story, a message, a connection.
