About

As someone who has been working for nearly 2 decades in the industry, I've seen many trends come and go, and I've picked up a lot over the years.

I see design as a broad topic that touches every part of our lives, something organic and ever-changing that comes in many different guises. There is no one process or method in which it happens. The creative possibilities inherent in everything are what always fascinates me regardless of the industry or user demographic.

After all, I truly believe there is no boring way to make money, and every venture is a fun new playground in which to try brave new things. Change is the only constant. My goal is to always challenge everything and think critically. Never follow convention and define success on your terms.
